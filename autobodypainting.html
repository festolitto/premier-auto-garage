  2. Surface Repair

    3. Why surface repair? Have you seen those cracks, chips and peeling on your vehicle? If left unattended to for a while these conditions have the capability of ruining your paint job. Repairing such conditions helps in giving your vehicle the highest possible gloss and a smooth finish. At Brystar, we examine your vehicle and give the appropriate repair suggestions.     4. Has it ever crossed your mind why some paint jobs do not hold their shine after being done? It is simple. Mostly, it is caused by the new paint mixing with the old thus creating uneven gloss and colour. Applying a surface sealer ensures that a barrier is established between the old and new paint. This reciprocates by ensuring a higher gloss and consistent colour on the painted surface long after the job is done.
